What to expect & things to do

Experience the Vibrant Fall Colors: September in Breckenridge brings stunning displays of autumn foliage as the aspen trees turn golden yellow. Take scenic drives along routes like Boreas Pass Road or hike through the surrounding trails to immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of the changing leaves.

Outdoor Adventures: With mild temperatures and clear skies, September is an ideal time for outdoor activities in Breckenridge. Enjoy hiking, mountain biking, or horseback riding on the extensive network of trails. You can also try your hand at fly fishing in the area's pristine rivers and streams.

Attend Festivals and Events: Breckenridge hosts several festivals and events in September, celebrating everything from music and art to food and culture. Don't miss the Breckenridge Wine Classic, where you can sample wines from around the world amidst stunning mountain views, or the Breckenridge Film Festival showcasing independent films from talented filmmakers.

Explore Historic Downtown: Stroll through the charming streets of historic downtown Breckenridge, lined with quaint shops, art galleries, and cozy cafes. Learn about the town's rich mining history by visiting museums and historic sites like the Barney Ford House Museum or the Country Boy Mine.

Adventure Park Activities: Head to the Breckenridge Adventure Park for adrenaline-pumping activities such as zip-lining, alpine slides, and ropes courses. It's a fantastic way to enjoy the crisp mountain air and stunning scenery while having fun with family and friends.

Relax and Unwind: After a day of exploration, unwind with a visit to one of Breckenridge's many spas or wellness centers. Treat yourself to a massage or yoga session to rejuvenate both body and mind amidst the serene mountain surroundings.

In September, Breckenridge offers a perfect blend of outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, and relaxation opportunities, making it an ideal destination for a memorable fall getaway.
